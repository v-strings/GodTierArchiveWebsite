LTG Clips Archive
==========

Large Discord Clips Archive [179 Videos]:
https://archive.org/details/randomltgclips

Most of these were found through discord and should be under 8mb, with some additional ones mixed in that Stainbine clipped.

---------------

If you're looking to make LowTierGod compilations with some basic editing, there's a massive archive of clips Stainbine has clipped and sorted by resolution and his (subjective) rating of entertainment value. Some editing is recommended to reduce the dead air and beginning and end, ffmpeg and a vegas crack are good options.
